Overview

This website describes the activities conducted for each of the phases of this research.

Study area

The study area was defined as the intersection of Colombia international boundary and the Llanos savannas ecoregion (Olson and Dinerstein 1998, Olson et al., 2001) identified by World Wildlife Fund (WWF).

Phase 1: Spatial dynamics and drivers of land-cover change

This phase seeks to determine the socioeconomic and environmental factors driving land use and land cover change (LULCC). The Phase 1 section describes the data sources, methods and results for this stage of the research.

Phase 2: Impacts of land-cover change

The goal of this phase is to assess the main consequences of LULCC on key ecosystem processes in the Llanos. The Phase 2 section describes the data sources, methods and results for this stage of the research.

Phase 3: Future scenarios of land-cover change and corresponding impacts

Phase 3 will evaluate future scenarios of land-cover change and their impacts on the Llanos ecosystems. The Phase 3 section describes the data sources, methods and results for this stage of the research.
