Phase 1: Spatial dynamics and drivers of land-cover change

The first phase of this research is the analysis of the spatial dynamic and drivers of land-cover change.

Data sources

  • Landsat 5 TM, Landsat 7 ETM+ and Landsat 8 OLI/TIRS imagery from USGS.
  • WRS-2 descending grid.

Methods

Image selection

  • Landsat imagery was selected for 12 tiles for the time period between 2000 and 2016 for a total of 204 Landsat scenes. One Landsat image was selected for each tile for each year. Landsat 5 and Landsat 7 imagery was selected for years 2000 to 2011. Landsat 8 images were used for years 2013 to 2016.
  • Landsat scenes with low or none cloud cover over the study area were preferred. Landsat 5 and Landsat 7 SLC-on scenes were preferred over Landsat 7 SLC-off scenes. Images taken during the dry season (January-March) were preferred over images taken in other months over the year.
  • Landsat 5 and Landsat 7 imagery was selected using the USGS EarthExplorer. Landsat 8 imagery was selected using the Libra browser.
  • The complete list of the selected Landsat imagery can be seen here. This list also shows progress in image processing and offers links to each scene thumbnail.

Image preparation

  • Images were preprocessed using the CLASlite software.
